    Welcome!

    Welcome to the Audio Visual Tour of the NYU Abu Dhabi - a place we call our Home!

    Are you physically away from the campus, but have it in your heart? Do you miss the liveliness of marketplace while you eat, the chirping at Palms during sunset, the enthusiasm at the jym, or the soothing energy at the highline?

    OR Do you want to experience how life on campus is?

    This tour takes you on a uniquely immersive virtual ride across our Campus!

    Step 1: Choose from a category of following locations on campus:

    • Outdoors
    • Study Spots
    • Dining and Fitness

    Step 2: Explore the map and choose where you want to be

    Step 3: Immerse yourself in the 360 Degree view and sounds from the location!

    OUTDOORS

    WELCOME CENTER

    Welcome Center is your official welcome to the campus. It's the premier entrance to the campus premises. It is also your gateway to the city.

    THE PALMS

    The signature 36 palms of NYUAD welcome all visitors on campus. The palms stand strong in the face of the cruel Abu dhabi weather and are aligned in perfect harmony; this depicts the resilience.

    HIGHLINE

    The high Line serves the highway to NYUAD residential colleges, linking them all. It connects the campus like none other. Evenings and nights are often perfects times to take a walk, study, or chill with friends at the high line.

    STUDY SPOTS

    LIBRARY

    Located on the top floor of the Campus Center, the NYU Abu Dhabi Library provides spaces for engagement between faculty and students, complemented by quiet areas for concentration and contemplation.

    LIBRARY CAFE

    The aroma of coffee. The gentle warmth of sunshine through a glass window. Cellphone chargers. The Library Cafe has all you need to ace that upcoming test.

    MARKETPLACE

    The Marketplace could easily be considered the indoor central location of the campus. Want to try savory restaurants, pancakes or ice-cream, or want to grab a coffee and work on your project? Marketplace is the spot!

    DINING AND FITNESS

    D2

    This is the place to sample global favorites, including Arabic cuisines, stone-baked pizzas, grilled fish & meats, hot Asian inspired dishes, dedicated vegan and vegetarian counter, and a large self-serve salad bar.

    FITNESS CENTER

    The NYU Abu Dhabi Fitness Center is a state-of-the-art facility located in the Campus Center building. The facility is fully equipped with training equipment.

    Inspiration

    It is you who inspired us to the idea!

    The pandamic has taken alot on all of us.

    It may have changed our world in many ways, but a thing that remains constant is our love and sense of belonging to our home - The NYU Abu Dhabi Campus

    While some have access to life on campus, many of our friends and community members miss being at their favorite spots on campus.

    We made this Audio-visual experience of our campus, to bring the campus experience to your screen. Just choose your spot, and play it's sound in your background. With a 360 Degree view, and sounds from the location, you can feel you are at the D2 while you eat, at the Library Cafe while you study, and at the Fitness Center while you workout!
